## ✅ Week 1-2: Data Cleaning & EDA

**Datasets:** Titanic & Housing Prices

**Tasks Completed:**
- Loaded and explored both datasets
- Handled missing values (Age, Embarked, Cabin)
- Removed duplicates
- Performed univariate & bivariate analysis
- Created 11 individual visualizations + 2 dashboards
- Generated correlation heatmaps
- Identified key predictors for survival (Titanic) and price (Housing)

**Key Insights:**
- **Titanic:** Survival rate 38.4%. Women and 1st class passengers survived at much higher rates. Top predictor: Pclass (-0.34)
- **Housing:** Area is the strongest price predictor (0.54), followed by bathrooms (0.52) and stories (0.42)
